# Experience

My work combines artificial intelligence research, engineering, and leadership across startups, academia, and technical student organisations.

---

## Current Experience

### XISTRA *— Founder*
*2025 – Present*

XISTRA is a **deep-tech AI startup** built around a single thesis: true intelligence emerges from the capacity to learn autonomously, not from data alone. Current AI systems are trained rigidly on existing patterns; they cannot explore their environment, adapt to new situations, or improve their own performance through experience.

Our mission is to change that: to build AI that enables robotic systems to **learn from their surroundings** and maximise performance in dynamic, previously unseen environments. We position ourselves fundamentally as a research company, with the goal of translating laboratory advances into real-world robots, starting with drones given their versatility and broad applicability.

We are at an early stage. My focus is research into **world models and training methods**, building the technical foundation for our first commercial proposal.  
[LinkedIn](https://www.linkedin.com/company/xistra)

### ATRG *— Researcher*
*November 2024 – Present*

ATRG (Aerospace Technology Research Group) is a research group at the University of Vigo with **over 17 years of experience** and high-impact space missions, including XatCobeo, Spain's first nanosatellite, HUMESAT-D, and Lume-1.

My contribution focuses on **artificial intelligence**, developing and researching AI model applications for drones: advanced data analysis algorithms, LLM integration for sensor data interpretation, and image recognition models, within projects spanning both the aeronautical and space domains.  
[LinkedIn](https://www.linkedin.com/company/atrg)

### DARTS *— Founder*
*February 2026 – Present*

DARTS is an **open research initiative** working toward AI that genuinely understands its environment, builds its own model of the world, and generates new knowledge rather than recombining existing patterns. We focus on three areas where we believe the next leap forward will happen: **world models, causal & continual learning, and multi-agent systems**.

All of our work is developed in the open: DARTS serves as a unified portal bringing together advances, developments, and research across these lines, fully open-source and public.  
[Webpage](https://dartsinitiative.com)
