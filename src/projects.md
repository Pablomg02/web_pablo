# Experience

My work combines artificial intelligence research, engineering, and leadership across startups, academia, and technical student organisations.

---

## Current Experience

### XISTRA
*2025 – Present*

XISTRA is a **deep-tech AI startup** researching and developing software and hardware technology that enables robotic systems—such as drones and autonomous vehicles—to operate reliably in the real world. Our core mission is to push beyond the limitations of current advanced models like LLMs, which are trained rigidly by imitating existing data and struggle to truly understand their environment, execute continuous missions, or **learn from experience**.

We are currently in the early stage, working intensively to demonstrate how we can contribute to the broader pursuit of AI that genuinely understands.  
[LinkedIn](https://www.linkedin.com/company/xistra)

### ATRG
*November 2024 – Present*

ATRG (Aerospace Technology Research Group) is a research group at the University of Vigo with **over 17 years of experience** and high-impact space missions, including XatCobeo—Spain's first nanosatellite—HUMESAT-D, and Lume-1.

My contribution focuses on **artificial intelligence**, developing and researching AI model applications for drones: advanced data analysis algorithms, LLM integration for sensor data interpretation, and image recognition models, within projects spanning both the aeronautical and space domains.  
[LinkedIn](https://www.linkedin.com/company/atrg)

### DARTS
*February 2026 – Present*

DARTS is an **open research initiative** working toward AI that genuinely understands its environment, builds its own model of the world, and generates new knowledge rather than recombining existing patterns. We focus on three areas where we believe the next leap forward will happen: **world models, causal & continual learning, and multi-agent systems**.

All of our work is developed in the open—DARTS serves as a unified portal bringing together advances, developments, and research across these lines, fully open-source and public.  
[Webpage](https://dartsinitiative.com)

## Previous Experience

### Aguia Advanced Analytics

During this time, I worked on the modelling and implementation of **computer vision neural networks**, as well as data processing and process automation, gaining first-hand experience in the development of an early-stage R&D project.  
[Webpage](https://aguian.com/)

### UVigo Aerotech
*September 2021 – August 2024*

UVigo Aerotech is a student team at the University of Vigo—the only one of its kind in Galicia—dedicated to developing aerospace technologies for research and competition. During my time with the team, I led participation in **5 fixed-wing drone competitions**, developing 5 different aircraft including MOBULA-0, **Spain's first competition flying wing**.

I also founded the **Research & Development group**, focused on pioneering technologies such as experimental AI models, morphing wing materials, and simulation software to support the rest of the team. I currently remain involved as an advisor.  
[Webpage](https://uvigoaerotech.com/)

Additionally, I contributed to optimising the development of the car's **aeromap**, reducing the time required and improving fidelity through statistical and modelling techniques.
