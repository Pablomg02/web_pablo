There are no articles here yet, but they will look like this one. This page is just a placeholder to show the style: how the title, date, tags, headings, equations and references will be presented.

## Headings and prose

Each article is written like a short lesson. Sections break the text into digestible parts, and the prose stays close to plain language even when the topic is technical. Key terms appear in *italics* the first time they show up.

## Equations

Mathematics is rendered with LaTeX, both inline &mdash; like $f(x) = \int_{0}^{x} e^{-t^2}\, dt$ &mdash; and as centred blocks:

$$
\nabla_\theta J(\theta) = \mathbb{E}_{\pi_\theta}\!\left[\nabla_\theta \log \pi_\theta(a \mid s)\, Q^{\pi_\theta}(s, a)\right].
$$

## Lists and references

Articles will often include short lists of ideas:

- A clear statement of the problem.
- The reasoning, step by step.
- Pointers to where you can read more.

And they will cite their sources at the end, so claims can be traced back.
